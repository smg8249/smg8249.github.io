We chose this project since Simran is from Southern California and regularly would listen to Jack FM.

Prior to reading about Lonergan’s work, they didn’t know about Jack FM’s unusual nature. We thought it was interesting that there was no discernible pattern to the song selection.

It seemed to mainly be hits from the 80s to the time the project took place in 2008. However there did not seem to be a reason why a certain song was played a certain number of times. .
